Introduction

This is an introduction to building your own custom computer to fit your needs. Building your own PC is the way to go if you want the best performance per dollar, and at the best value. Despite what other people may tell you, it is straightforward and easy to do. If this is your first time building a custom computer, building custom computers simply involves selecting and buying all your computer components separately and then installing them one by one. If you choose the right parts you will end up with a fast, cost-effective custom computer capable of running modern applications flawlessly and quickly
